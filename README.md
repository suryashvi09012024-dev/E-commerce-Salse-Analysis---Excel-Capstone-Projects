## 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, customer behavior, and cost trends across different regions and product categories.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 💰 Sales Performance
- Revenue is unevenly distributed across sub-categories.
- A few products contribute significantly to overall sales, while others underperform.

### 🌍 Regional Analysis
- North and South regions show the highest sales volume.
- West region has comparatively lower performance, indicating growth opportunities.

### 📅 Monthly Cost Trends
- Costs remain relatively stable throughout the year.
- Slight increases are observed during peak months, suggesting seasonal demand.

### 🛍️ Category Insights
- Sports and Electronics categories have higher unit prices (premium segment).
- Beauty category has lower pricing, indicating budget-friendly products.

### 👥 Customer Insights
- Platinum loyalty customers contribute the most to stock engagement.
- Discount distribution is nearly equal across genders, showing balanced promotional strategies.

---

## 🎯 Business Value
- Identifies high-performing products and regions  
- Helps optimize pricing and discount strategies  
- Supports better inventory and stock planning  
- Enables targeted customer segmentation
